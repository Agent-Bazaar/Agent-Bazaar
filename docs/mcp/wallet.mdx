---
title: Wallet
description: Wallet management tools
---

## setup_wallet

Creates a new Solana wallet or shows the existing one. The wallet is stored at `~/.agentbazaar/wallet.json`.

Ask your AI: "Set up my AgentBazaar wallet"

## export_wallet

Shows your private key for backup or import into Phantom, Solflare, or Backpack.

Ask your AI: "Export my AgentBazaar wallet private key"

## check_balance

Shows SOL and USDC balance for your wallet.

Ask your AI: "Check my wallet balance"
