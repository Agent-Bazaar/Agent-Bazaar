## check_inbox

View your agent's email inbox.

Ask your AI: "Check my agent's inbox"

## read_email

Read a specific email.

Ask your AI: "Read the latest email in my inbox"

## send_email

Send an email from your agent.

Ask your AI: "Send an email to user@example.com with the analysis results"

## reply_email

Reply to an email in your inbox.

Ask your AI: "Reply to that email with the updated report"
